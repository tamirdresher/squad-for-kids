## YouTube Video Template

### Intro (3 seconds)
- Deep Navy background
- "TechAI Explained" in Electric Cyan, centered
- Subtle glow animation on text
- No sound or soft whoosh

### Slide Template
- Background: Deep Navy (`#0a0a2e`)
- Title bar: Electric Cyan text on slightly lighter navy (`#12122e`)
- Body text: White
- Code blocks: Dark Card background with JetBrains Mono
- Diagrams: Cyan + Magenta + Gold accent colors
- Bottom bar: "TechAI Explained | @TDSquadAITeam" in Soft Gray
- Slide number in bottom-right corner

### Outro (5 seconds)
- "Subscribe & Hit the Bell 🔔" in Electric Cyan
- "@TDSquadAITeam" in Hot Magenta
- "No fluff, just signal." in Soft Gray
- Links to Gumroad and blog

### Thumbnail Template
- Bold white text (2-4 words MAX)
- Electric Cyan or Hot Magenta accent element
- Dark gradient background
- Optional: small tech icon or emoji
- NO face (faceless channel)
- Consistent font and layout across all thumbnails

## Voice & Tone

### DO
- "Here's what actually matters..."
- "Let me show you the real-world pattern..."
- "Skip the theory, here's how it works in production..."
- Use analogies developers relate to
- Include code examples when relevant
- Be opinionated — recommend specific tools

### DON'T
- "In this video we're going to..."
- Marketing speak or hype words
- Clickbait without substance
- Generic introductions
- Apologize for content length

## Cross-Promotion Rules

Every piece of content should include AT LEAST ONE:
- Link to a Gumroad product (when relevant)
- Link to another YouTube video (end screen)
- Link to the blog
- Newsletter signup CTA
- Affiliate link (when recommending tools)

## File Naming Convention

- Videos: `YYYY-MM-DD-topic-slug.mp4`
- Thumbnails: `YYYY-MM-DD-topic-slug-thumb.png`
- Articles: `topic-slug.md`
- Products: descriptive kebab-case
