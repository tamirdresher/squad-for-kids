# Pricing Strategy — AI Engineering Squad Course

## Udemy Pricing Tiers Analysis

### How Udemy Pricing Works

| Tier | List Price | Typical Sale Price | Instructor Revenue (Organic) | Instructor Revenue (Promo) |
|------|-----------|-------------------|------------------------------|---------------------------|
| Free | $0 | $0 | $0 | $0 |
| Low | $19.99-$29.99 | $9.99-$14.99 | 37% of list | 37% of sale |
| Mid | $49.99-$84.99 | $12.99-$19.99 | 37% organic / 97% coupon | 37% organic |
| Premium | $89.99-$199.99 | $12.99-$24.99 | 37% organic / 97% coupon | 37% organic |

### Key Udemy Revenue Rules
- **Organic sales (Udemy finds the student):** Instructor gets **37%** of the sale price
- **Instructor coupon / referral link sales:** Instructor gets **97%** of the sale price
- **Udemy Deals / promotions:** Udemy can discount to as low as **$9.99**
- **Subscription (Udemy Business):** Revenue from pool based on consumption minutes

### Revenue Split Implications
The massive difference between 37% and 97% means **driving your own traffic is 2.6x more profitable per sale** than relying on Udemy's marketplace.

```
Stage 1: FREE CHEAT SHEET (Lead Magnet)
    ↓ (Kit automated sequence)
Stage 2: $49.99 LAUNCH DISCOUNT (first 100 enrollments)
    ↓ (expires after 2 weeks)
Stage 3: $59.99 EVERGREEN COUPON (ongoing referral link)
    ↓ (for non-converters)
```

**"The Multi-Agent AI Architecture Cheat Sheet"**
