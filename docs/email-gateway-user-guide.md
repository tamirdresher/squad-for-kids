# 📧 How to Send Requests via Email

> This is your personal gateway to get things done! Just send an email and it happens automatically.

---

## The Email Address

Send all requests to:

> **tamir.requests@yourdomain.com**
> 
> *(Tamir will share the exact address with you)*

---

## What You Can Do

### 🖨️ Print Something

Put **"print"** in the subject line and attach the file.

**Example:**
- **To:** tamir.requests@yourdomain.com
- **Subject:** `print grocery list`
- **Attach:** the file you want printed (PDF, Word, photo, etc.)

The file will be sent to the home printer automatically.

---

### 📅 Add to Calendar

Put **"calendar"**, **"meeting"**, **"schedule"**, or **"event"** in the subject line.

**Example:**
- **To:** tamir.requests@yourdomain.com
- **Subject:** `calendar Dentist appointment`
- **Body:** `Tuesday January 21 at 3:00 PM, about 1 hour`

An event will be created on Tamir's calendar. He may adjust the exact time.

---

### ✅ Create a Reminder or Task

Put **"remind"**, **"todo"**, **"task"**, or **"remember"** in the subject line.

**Example:**
- **To:** tamir.requests@yourdomain.com
- **Subject:** `remind Pick up dry cleaning tomorrow`
- **Body:** *(optional — add more details here)*

A task will appear in Tamir's To Do list.

---

### 💬 Anything Else (General Request)

If your email doesn't have any of the keywords above, it becomes a general request that Tamir's team will see and handle.

**Example:**
- **To:** tamir.requests@yourdomain.com
- **Subject:** `Can you fix the kitchen light?`
- **Body:** `The one above the sink has been flickering.`

---

## Quick Reference

| What you want | Put this word in the subject |
|---------------|------------------------------|
| Print a file | **print** |
| Calendar event | **calendar**, **meeting**, **schedule**, or **event** |
| Reminder/task | **remind**, **todo**, **task**, or **remember** |
| Anything else | Just write normally — no special word needed |

---

## Tips

- ✅ You'll get a **confirmation email** when your request is processed
- 📎 For printing, **attach the file** to the email
- 📅 For calendar events, include the **date, time, and how long** in the email body
- ⏱️ Requests are usually processed within **1–5 minutes**
- 📧 Only emails from **your email address** are accepted (security measure)

## Something Not Working?

- Make sure you're sending from **your usual email address**
- Check the **subject line** has the right keyword
- For printing, make sure the **file is attached** (not just a link)
- If something still isn't working, just email Tamir directly 😊
