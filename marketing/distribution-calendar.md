## CONTINGENCY

### If Engagement is Low (Week 1-2)

**Possible Causes:**
- Wrong audience segment
- Timing (holidays, competing news, etc.)
- Messaging doesn't resonate

**Response:**
1. Analyze which content DID get engagement
2. Double down on that angle in Week 3
3. Consider different hooks (more technical? more personal?)
4. Check timing (post at different hours)

### If One Piece Explodes (100+ retweets, etc.)

**Action:**
1. Create follow-up thread expanding on that idea
2. Prepare for flood of incoming traffic
3. Have thoughtful replies ready for common questions
4. Alert Geordi for real-time analytics
