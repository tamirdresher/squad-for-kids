## вҡЎ How to Start in 30 Seconds вҖ” Fork вҶ’ Hello вҶ’ Done!

Getting started with Kids Squad is as easy as 1-2-3. Seriously вҖ” 30 seconds.

### Step 1: Fork рҹҚҙ

1. Go to the project page: **[github.com/tamirdresher/kids-squad-setup](https://github.com/tamirdresher/kids-squad-setup)**
2. Click the **Fork** button (top right)
3. Click **Create fork**

> **What's a Fork?** Think of it like photocopying a worksheet вҖ” you get your own copy that you can change without messing up the original.

### Step 2: Open Your Coding Environment рҹ–ҘпёҸ

The easiest way вҖ” **GitHub Codespace** (no installing anything on your computer!):
1. On your Fork page, click the green **Code** button
2. Switch to the **Codespaces** tab
3. Click **Create codespace on main**
4. Wait about a minute for it to load вҸі

> **What's a Codespace?** It's a coding environment that runs in the cloud вҖ” right in your browser. Nothing to install. Just click and code.

**Alternative вҖ” Local VS Code:**
1. Install [VS Code](https://code.visualstudio.com/) (free)
2. Install the [GitHub Copilot](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=GitHub.copilot) extension
3. Clone your Fork and open it in VS Code

### Step 3: Say Hello! рҹ‘Ӣ

1. Open **Copilot Chat** (the chat panel on the side, or press `Ctrl+Shift+I`)
2. Type: **Hello**
3. That's it! The AI will guide you step by step рҹҺү

```
рҹ’¬ You: Hello
рҹӨ– Copilot: Hi there! Welcome to Kids Squad! рҹҺү
   I'm your team. Tell me a bit about yourself:
   - What's your name?
   - How old are you?
   - What are you interested in? (Games? Websites? Science?)
```

**Fork вҶ’ Open вҶ’ "Hello" вҶ’ Your team is live!** вң…

## рҹҶ“ How to Get Copilot for Free

There are two ways to get Copilot for free. Let's walk through both.

### Way 1: Copilot Free вҖ” For Everyone (Fastest вҡЎ)

GitHub Copilot Free is available to **anyone with a GitHub account** вҖ” no special requirements.

**What you get:**
- вң… ~2,000 code completions per month
- вң… 50 chat messages per month
- вң… Works in VS Code, Codespaces, and on github.com
- вң… Multilingual support (English, Hebrew, Spanish, and more)

**How to activate:**
1. Go to [github.com](https://github.com) and sign up (or log in)
2. Go to [github.com/features/copilot](https://github.com/features/copilot)
3. Click **Get started for free**
4. Done! Copilot is now active on your account

> **рҹ’Ў Tip:** 50 chat messages per month is plenty for a kid coding a few times a week. Ask detailed questions to get the most out of each message!

**What if you run out of messages?** The kids-squad-setup repo has a [free alternatives guide](https://github.com/tamirdresher/kids-squad-setup/blob/main/copilot-free-tier-fallback.md) вҖ” ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude all work great too!

### Way 2: Student Developer Pack вҖ” Free Copilot Pro for Students рҹҺ“

If you're a **student** (age 13+), you can get **Copilot Pro for free** вҖ” unlimited chat, no restrictions! It's part of the GitHub Student Developer Pack, which includes premium tools worth hundreds of dollars.

#### What you need

- вң… Age 13 or older
- вң… A GitHub account
- вң… Currently enrolled in school (middle school, high school, college вҖ” any counts)
- вң… Two-factor authentication (2FA) enabled on your account
- вң… Proof of student status (school email or document)

#### The Guide вҖ” Step by Step

##### Step 1: Create a GitHub Account рҹ‘Ө

1. Go to [github.com/signup](https://github.com/signup)
2. Enter an email address (if you have a school email like `yourname@school.edu` вҖ” great! It speeds up verification)
3. Choose a username and password
4. Verify your email (click the link sent to your inbox)

##### Step 2: Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) рҹ”җ

This is required! Your application won't be approved without it.

1. Go to **Settings** in your profile menu (click your avatar вҶ’ Settings)
2. Click **Password and authentication**
3. Under **Two-factor authentication**, click **Enable**
4. Choose a method:
   - **Authenticator app** (recommended) вҖ” like Google Authenticator or Microsoft Authenticator
   - **SMS** вҖ” text message to your phone
5. Save your **recovery codes** somewhere safe!

> **рҹ’Ў For parents:** Help your kids set up 2FA. Use the Authenticator app on your phone and keep the recovery codes stored safely.

##### Step 3: Prepare Proof of Student Status рҹ“„

You'll need one of these:
- **School email** вҖ” if you have a school-issued email address (like `yourname@school.edu`), this is the fastest way
- **Student ID card** вҖ” take a clear photo. Make sure your name and expiration date are visible
- **Enrollment letter** вҖ” a document from your school showing you're currently enrolled
- **Class schedule** вҖ” with your name and current dates

> **рҹ’Ў Tip:** The document must show your **name** and the **current school year**.

##### Step 4: Submit Your Application рҹ“¬

1. Go to **[education.github.com/pack](https://education.github.com/pack)**
2. Click **Get your pack** вҶ’ **Sign up for Student Developer Pack**
3. Fill out the form:
   - Search for your school by name
   - Indicate when you expect to graduate
   - Upload your proof (school email or document)
4. Click **Submit**

##### Step 5: Wait for Approval вҸі

- Usually takes a few days
- GitHub sends an email when your application is approved
- If denied вҖ” read the notes, and resubmit with a clearer document

##### Step 6: Enjoy! рҹҺү

Once approved, you get:
- вң… **Copilot Pro** вҖ” unlimited chat messages!
- вң… **Cloud credits** вҖ” Azure, DigitalOcean, and more
- вң… **Free domain name** вҖ” build a real website!
- вң… **Premium tools** вҖ” design tools, databases, and tons more

### FAQ

**Q: I'm 10 years old вҖ” can I use this?**
A: Absolutely! Copilot Free works at any age. The Student Pack requires age 13+. Until then, 50 chat messages per month is plenty for cool projects.

**Q: My parents aren't tech-savvy. Is that okay?**
A: Totally fine! The Fork вҶ’ Codespace вҶ’ Hello workflow works without any parent involvement. If you need help with 2FA, just ask them to install an Authenticator app вҖ” that's all.

**Q: Does it work in other languages?**
A: Yes! Squad speaks great Hebrew, Spanish, and many other languages. You can even mix languages вҖ” start in English, switch to your native language whenever you want.

**Q: What if I run out of chat messages?**
A: Three options: 1) Wait for next month (resets on the 1st), 2) Use ChatGPT/Gemini/Claude for free, 3) Apply for the Student Developer Pack.

**Q: Can I use it on my phone?**
A: Codespace works in any browser вҖ” including your phone! But a bigger screen is definitely more comfortable.

**Q: Can it help me study for tests?**
A: Yes вҖ” the team helps you understand material and creates practice problems. It won't give you answers to copy.

**Q: Is this safe for kids?**
A: Squad runs inside GitHub's infrastructure with their safety guardrails. The age-adapted mode keeps interactions appropriate. That said, parents вҖ” you're always welcome to sit alongside and see what your kids are building. It's actually pretty fun to watch. рҹҷӮ
