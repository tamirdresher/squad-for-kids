## Troubleshooting

**Can't find game in dashboard?**
- Click "All Games" on left sidebar
- Search by title in the list
- Verify you're logged in with correct account

**Tags not saving?**
- Click "Save changes" button after editing
- Check for validation errors in red text
- Limit to 10-15 most relevant tags

**Collection doesn't appear?**
- Collections take ~10 minutes to update
- Refresh page after saving
- Check collection visibility settings (public vs private)

**External links not working?**
- Verify full URL (include https://)
- Test link in new tab to confirm
- Save and refresh to see changes
