## Best Practices

- **Disclose clearly** — FTC requires it, and transparency builds trust
- **Only link what you use** — Tamir's credibility is his biggest asset
- **Use deep links** — Link to specific products, not homepages
- **Track performance** — Use UTM parameters and affiliate dashboards
- **Don't overdo it** — 2-3 affiliate links per post maximum
- **Create "resources" pages** — Evergreen content with curated affiliate links converts well
