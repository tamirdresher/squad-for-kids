## 🎓 KEY INSIGHTS FROM RESEARCH

### What Kids Actually Spend On
- Cosmetics (skins, emotes, effects)
- Battle passes (seasonal, time-limited)
- Content packs (questions, meme unlocks)
- NOT ads, NOT subscriptions alone

### Why Brainrot Is Addictive
- Hyper-short format (3-15 sec = infinite scroll)
- In-group slang = tribal identity ("skibidi," "sigma," "gyatt")
- Self-aware irony = kids know it's ridiculous, that's the appeal
- Algorithm amplification = TikTok/YouTube prioritizes engagement (brainrot = high engagement)
- Creator-friendly = AI tools make remixing easy

### Successful Monetization Models (Validated)
- **Roblox:** $14M+/week (user-generated + cosmetics + social)
