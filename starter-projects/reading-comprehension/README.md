## Who is this for?

This guide is designed for **6th grade students** preparing for an English reading comprehension test. It covers:

- 🎯 **Finding the main idea** — strategies and practice
- 🔍 **Finding specific details** — annotation techniques and where to look
- 🃏 **Multiple choice strategies** — the elimination method and answer traps
- 📖 **Vocabulary tips** — context clues, word roots, and Unit 5 vocabulary strategies

---

## How to use the guide

### 1. Start with the strategy sections (top of the guide)
Read through all four strategy sections before touching the practice passages. Hermione explains each skill with examples and tips.

### 2. Try the practice passages on your own
- Read the passage fully once
- Then read the questions
- Go back and annotate (underline, circle, star)
- Answer the questions without looking at the answer key

### 3. Check the answer key
The answer key doesn't just give answers — it **explains why** each answer is correct. Read the explanations even for questions you got right!

### 4. Use the Before-the-Test Checklist
The last section of the guide has a checklist for the night before, morning of, and during the test. Print it out or copy it into a notebook!

## Tips from Hermione

- 📝 Always have a pencil handy when reading — annotating is not optional!
- 🔍 Read the questions **before** the passage so you know what to look for
- ✂️ Eliminate wrong answers — you don't just pick the right answer, you rule out the wrong ones
- 🔁 **Always go back to the passage** to check your answer
