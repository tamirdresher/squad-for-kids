## What This Area Does

The research area is the **knowledge base and investigation hub** for the tamresearch1 project.
It contains academic papers, AI model surveys, voice/TTS research, book content, and ongoing
investigation documents that inform decisions across all other areas.

Key responsibilities:
- **Active research** (`research/active/`) — in-progress investigations and open questions
- **Book content** — full draft chapters for *The Squad System* book (Markdown + PDF outputs)
- **AI model surveys** — comparative analysis of LLMs and AI services (e.g., `ai-models-survey-2026-03-21.md`)
- **Voice / TTS research** — academic papers and audio experiments for podcast generation
- **Issue-linked research** — files named `{issue-number}-*.md` contain research backing specific issues
- **AZD AI Agent integration research** — `azd-aiagent-squad-integration-2026-03-21.md`
- **Book images** — visual assets for book chapters under `book-images/`

Research documents are **inputs**, not outputs — they inform code, infrastructure, and content
decisions but are not deployed or published directly.

## Routing Hints

- **New research documents** → Seven authors; no approval required for drafts
- **Issue-linked research** (`{issue#}-*.md`) → Seven; tag the corresponding GitHub issue
- **Book chapter content** → Seven authors; Picard reviews for accuracy and scope
- **AI model surveys** → Seven; share findings summary in relevant issues when complete
- **Voice / TTS experiments** → Seven primary; coordinate with scripts area for tooling
- **Research backing architectural decisions** → Seven produces, Picard consumes and signs off in ADR
- **Published/exported PDFs** → Seven generates; do not edit PDFs directly, edit source `.md` files

## Constraints

- Research documents are drafts — do not treat content as authoritative until promoted to an ADR or decision record
- Issue-linked files must reference their issue number in the filename (`{issue#}-{slug}.md`)
- Book PDF exports are generated artifacts — source of truth is the corresponding `.md` files
- Do not commit large binary assets (audio, video) without Picard approval — use external storage instead
- `research/active/` is for open investigations only; move completed research to the area root

## Conventions

- Filename pattern for issue research: `{issue-number}-{brief-slug}.md` (e.g., `1289-monorepo-layer1.md`)
- Book chapters follow `book-chapter{N}-draft.md` → `book-chapter{N}-styled.md` pipeline
- Audio files (`.mp3`) are experiments only; the canonical research output is the `.md` companion file
- All dates in filenames use `YYYY-MM-DD` format
