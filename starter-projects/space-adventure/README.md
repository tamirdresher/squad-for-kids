# 🚀 Space Adventure Game

**A Star Wars-themed space game for young Rebel pilots!**

---

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser (double-click it, or drag it into Chrome/Edge/Firefox)
2. Click **BEGIN MISSION**
3. Pilot your spaceship to dodge the falling asteroids!

---

## Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| ⬅️ Left Arrow | Move ship left |
| ➡️ Right Arrow | Move ship right |
| ⬆️ Up Arrow | Move ship up |
| ⬇️ Down Arrow | Move ship down |

You can also use **W / A / S / D** keys — same as in video games!

---

## Rules

- You start with **3 lives** ❤️❤️❤️
- Every second you survive = **+1 point** ⭐
- If an asteroid hits your ship, you **lose a life**
- After getting hit, you have a brief **invincibility flash** (use it wisely!)
- The game gets **harder over time** — asteroids speed up!
- When all lives are gone → **Game Over**

---

## Tips for Winning 🏆

- Stay near the **middle** so you can dodge in both directions
- Watch for **two asteroids at once** — they appear when your score gets high!
- The asteroids get **faster** as your score increases — stay focused!
