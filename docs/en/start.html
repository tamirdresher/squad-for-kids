🟢 Start in 3 Steps

In 5 minutes your child will be talking to their personal AI teaching team

①
⏱️ 2 minutes

Create an Account

Go to github.com and click Sign Up

Pick a username, email, and password.
Just like signing up for any website.

💡 Child over 13? Let them sign up on their own.
Under 13? A parent signs up and adds the child.
✅ Got an account — what's next?
②
⏱️ 1 minute

Open the Learning Window

Click the big green button:

🚀 Click Here to Open the Learning Window

Wait 30 seconds for it to load.

ℹ️ You'll see a window that looks like a text editor — that's normal!
That's your child's workspace.
✅ Loaded? Great!
③
⏱️ 30 seconds

Your Child Starts Talking

On the right side there's a chat window.
Your child types:

"Hi, my name is Daniel and I'm 9 years old"

The AI teacher will ask a few questions:
What do you like? What grade are you in?
Then it builds a personalized team.

✅ That's it! Your child is ready to learn 🎉

🎉 That's All!

Your child is already talking to their team. Amazing, right?
