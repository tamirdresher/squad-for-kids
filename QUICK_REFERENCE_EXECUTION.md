## Failure Modes & Pivot

**If Week 1 views < 10K:**
- Check: Are posts actually live on all platforms?
- Check: Are people clicking through?
- Pivot: Increase conversation seeding on Twitter
- Pivot: Expand newsletter outreach to 20+ targets

**If HN submission doesn't gain traction:**
- Not uncommon (70% of submissions don't hit top 30)
- Resubmit in 2 weeks with different wording
- Focus energy on Dev.to/Hashnode/LinkedIn instead

**If newsletter outreach gets no response:**
- Expand to smaller, niche newsletters
- Try different subject line approach
- Shift to Twitter conversation seeding instead

**If communities perceive as spam:**
- Stop immediately
- Reassess tone (should be value-first, not promotional)
- Make fewer posts, more genuine engagement
