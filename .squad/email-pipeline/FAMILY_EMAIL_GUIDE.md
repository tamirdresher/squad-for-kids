# Squad Family Email Pipeline

## Overview
Tamir's family can send requests and messages to the **AI Squad** using a dedicated email address that is monitored by the team.

---

## Email Address

**📧 td-squad-ai-team@outlook.com**

This email is checked regularly by the Squad and all messages are triaged and processed.

---

## How It Works

### For Tamir's Family (Wife, etc.)
1. Send an email to: **td-squad-ai-team@outlook.com**
2. Include a clear subject line describing your request
3. Keep the message concise — the Squad will follow up if details are needed

### For the Squad (Ralph, Kes, etc.)
1. **New emails arrive** in the td-squad-ai-team@outlook.com inbox
2. **Kes (Communications)** monitors and triages incoming messages:
   - Flagging family emails with `[FAMILY]` or `[WIFE]` prefix
   - Creating GitHub issues for actionable requests
   - Forwarding time-sensitive items to Tamir
3. **Ralph (Work Monitor)** picks up GitHub issues created and tracks status
4. **Picard (Lead)** reviews decisions and escalates as needed

---

## Request Types

### ✅ Good Examples to Send
- **Task requests**: "Can you help me find X?" or "I need a document about Y"
- **Schedule questions**: "When is Tamir free on Friday?"
- **Status checks**: "How is the podcast project progressing?"
- **Idea suggestions**: "Have you considered trying Z?"

### ❌ Avoid Sending
- Personal messages that should go directly to Tamir
- Highly sensitive family matters (use direct messaging instead)
- Requests needing urgent response (call/text Tamir directly)

---

## Email Etiquette

| Field | Guidance |
|-------|----------|
| **Subject** | Be specific — e.g., "Request: Hebrew Podcast Version" not "Hi" |
| **Body** | Include context; assume you're emailing the entire Squad, not just one person |
| **Attachments** | Supported — include any reference materials needed |
| **Response Time** | Expect reply within 24–48 hours during business days |

- **Emails are monitored** by the Squad to ensure important requests aren't missed
- **GitHub issues created** from emails are visible to the Squad team (internal only)
- **Credentials**: Do NOT share passwords or sensitive data in this email
- **Encryption**: For highly sensitive messages, ask Tamir for a secure alternative
